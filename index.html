Hello! Welcome to to my Trivia website - a final project for CS50!

This website is designed for users who love playing trivia! Users can answer questions from various, or all, categories and add points to their scores.


In addition to answering questions and racking up points, users can also add their own trivia questions. This website depends upon the integrity of all users, as there is no "fact checker" when their questions and answers are submitted. (Though, I can remove questions deemed inaccurate, ambiguous, or inappropriate from the database.)


Scroll through to see all of the categories to choose from!
